ReklamoKo

Reklamoko is a complaint reporting system that helps residents easily complain using their computers or smartphone. It also makes it easier for the barangay officials to handle complaints. There are two kinds of users: admins (barangay officials) and regular users (residents). The admin can approve account registration, view complaints, approve complaints, rejecting complaints, and transfer complaints. The regular user can add complaints, edit complaints, and delete complaints.
